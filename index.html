Points of interest related to handmade lace

A map to share on websites of lace guilds, teachers, artists and collectors. Anyone can contribute, a team of volunteers moderates it centrally.

between a clustered and searchable map.

Select a blue marker for details of an object. Select an item from the search results to find its location on the map. Search results are restricted to the visible area of the map.

Zoom in/out or drag the map for more object in the region you are interested in. You can zoom in with the colored circles or general methods like the +/- buttons or mouse wheel. The numbers in the circles show how many markers they represent. Hovering over the circles reveals the bounds of the represented markers.

Get involved

Please help us to improve the map and its content. If you know how to, create an issue or pull request on lacemap.github.io, otherwise send a message to the LOKK moderator of the map, she understand Dutch, English, German and French. A new item on the map requires at least a name and a brick-and-mortar address. For the proper coordinates you can zoom in on the map and click on the intended structure. Please specify contact information for a museum, preferable a website, not an email address as there is no protection against robots. A short remark about the collection or opening hours is also possible.
