Suggest updates

Inform the LOKK map moderator with either of the

  • Dutch form
  • form translated into your own language.

Or use your github account to propose changes or new entries, start editing with the pencil icon and follow further instructions.

A new item on the map requires

  • A name of the point of interest.
  • A brick-and-mortar address.
  • Coordinates which you can find by zooming in on the map and clicking the intended structure.
  • Optional contact information should not be an email address as there is no protection against robots. A website is fine as a short remark about opening hours.
